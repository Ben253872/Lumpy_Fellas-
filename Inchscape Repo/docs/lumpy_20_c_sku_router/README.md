# Notebook 20: Strict-Cutoff Horizon SKU Router For C

Notebook 20 tests SKU-level expert routing for established recurring C while enforcing the final information cutoff.

The historical horizon runs February 2023 through July 2024, using information through October 2022 and a 3-month gap. Its first nine months select the candidate pool; its final nine months validate router margins, specialist concentration, and blend strength. The locked rule is refitted on the full known historical horizon and then applied to November 2024 through April 2026.

August through October 2024 and all final outcomes are unavailable to selection and routing.
