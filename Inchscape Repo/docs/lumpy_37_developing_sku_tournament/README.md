# Lumpy 37 - Developing-SKU Tournament

Notebook 37 targets the 26 developing SKUs with 12-23 months of history. It compares frozen block models, metadata analogues, blended candidates, SBA-TSB combinations and modest calibration across five historical folds before the official evaluation.

## Result

Historical selection locked a blend of 75% full-log hurdle forecast and 25%
subfamily analogue forecast with a 1.00 calibration scale. On the 24 positive
official developing SKUs, below-50 coverage improved from 1 to 2 and below-70
coverage from 5 to 12. Below-100 remained 16. Median WMAPE improved from 82.89%
to 69.46%, portfolio WMAPE from 85.96% to 84.17%, and bias from -62.41% to
-39.65%.

Notebook 37 is promoted as the developing-SKU champion. The next focused test
should retain this occurrence/analogue blend and investigate conservative
high-volume calibration or routing, since broad calibration did not win the
historical tournament and residual portfolio bias remains negative.
